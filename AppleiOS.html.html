Apple IOS

Apple's iOS is one of the most popular smartphone operating systems, second only to Android.
It runs on Apple hardware, including iPhones, iPad tablets and iPod Touch media players.
Signature features of iOS include the App Store where users buy apps and download free software,
an emphasis on security including strong encryption to limit what unauthorized users can extract from the phone
and a simple, streamlined interface with minimal hardware buttons.
